# LitZap

**Get paid by anyone, anywhere.** LitZap is the money app for Litecoin's LitVM — send and receive money by name, X, or Discord, in seconds.

![LitZap](/banner.png)

Sign in with an email or a social account, claim your `name.zap`, and start moving money on-chain — no seed phrase, no crypto experience required. Send to a friend, request from a client, or split a bill with a whole group.

And LitZap reaches people **before they've even joined**: pay someone by their X or Discord handle, and the money waits safely on-chain until they claim it — or returns to you automatically. That's how money should move: to a person, not a 42-character address.

<Callout type="info">
  Everything in these docs marked **Live** is deployed and verifiable on the LitVM (LiteForge) testnet today. Anything still ahead is marked **Roadmap** — we never claim it as shipped.
</Callout>

## What you can do

- **Sign in with email, Google, X, or Discord** — a secure, self-custodied wallet is created for you. No seed phrase to write down.
- **Claim your `name.zap`** — a handle people pay you at, resolved on-chain.
- **Send & request money** in **USDC** (and native zkLTC) on LitVM.
- **Pay anyone by their X / Discord / email** — even if they're not on LitZap yet. Held safely, auto-refunds if unclaimed.
- **Drops** — split money across a group, red-packet style.
- **Zapster** — your built-in money companion, guiding you through every action.

Every one of these is **live** on LiteForge today, backed by five deployed smart contracts you can verify yourself. See [Smart contracts](/contracts).
