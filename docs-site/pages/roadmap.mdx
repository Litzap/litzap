# Roadmap

Our north star: **let anyone get paid by anyone — and turn it into real money.** The roadmap follows the wedge → moat → magic path from [Why LitZap](/why-different).

<Callout type="info">
  Honest by default. Items marked **Live** are deployed and verifiable on LiteForge today. Everything else is planned work, in roughly the order we intend to tackle it — not a promise of dates.
</Callout>

## Live today

- Email / Google / X / Discord sign-in with self-custodied embedded wallets
- `name.zap` on-chain identity
- USDC & zkLTC payments and requests
- **Pay-by-social** escrow (X / Discord / email) with auto-refund
- Drops (group payments)
- Zapster, the in-app guide (agentic actions coming — see below)
- Five deployed contracts ([see Contracts](/contracts))

## Near term — stabilise & launch

- Hardening and polish across all flows for a confident **testnet launch**
- Verified X / Discord profile linking refinements
- Clearer claim discovery and notifications
- Ecosystem listing and Builders Program participation

## Mid term — the moat

- **Off-ramp:** convert USDC into local money (mobile money, bank transfer) through partners — the piece that makes LitZap real money, not just crypto
- **On-ramp:** simple ways to fund a LitZap balance
- **Receive from / send to other chains** for broader reach
- **Partner SDK & API:** let businesses and LitVM projects pay by name/handle, accept payments, and resolve identities (see [Build on LitZap](/build-on-litzap))

## Longer term — the magic

- **Agentic Zapster:** move money with plain language — split bills, schedule payments, open escrows — executed safely on top of primitives that already work
- **Savings & yield:** make idle balances productive, with the complexity hidden
- **Card & spend:** spend a LitZap balance in the real world

## Privacy

Private payments aren't available today — LitVM transactions are currently public, and we won't claim otherwise. When LitVM enables privacy, LitZap will support it, so you can choose to keep your balances and amounts private.

## Mainnet readiness

Before LitZap handles real value on mainnet, we intend to complete:

- A **third-party security audit** of the contracts
- Hardened key management for operational signers (multisig / KMS)
- Production-grade gas and verification infrastructure

We'd rather ship these in the right order than rush real money onto unaudited rails.
