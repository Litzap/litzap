# Why LitZap

## The mission

**Let anyone get paid by anyone — and turn it into real money.**

Freelancers, creators, and families in emerging and high-inflation economies get paid by clients and relatives abroad, and lose **5–10%** and several days to banks and remittance services every time. For millions of them, holding dollars and getting paid in stablecoins is already the most practical option — the only thing missing is an app that makes it as simple as sending a message.

LitZap is that app. We bring the next wave of everyday people onto Litecoin by making money feel human: you get paid by your name, your X, or your Discord — and the crypto disappears into the background.

## Our edge

**Pay people, not addresses.** You send to a person by their handle, and LitZap handles the wallet underneath. This reaches people who **aren't in crypto yet** — funds wait safely on-chain until the recipient signs up and verifies the handle, or auto-refund to the sender. Receiving money becomes as easy as getting a DM.

That single capability changes the equation: onboarding is no longer a barrier the recipient has to cross *before* they can be paid — getting paid *is* the onboarding.

## How we win: wedge → moat → magic

<Callout type="info">
  We're deliberate about what's a **feature** (copyable in a weekend) versus a **moat** (hard, durable, defensible). We don't pretend features are moats.
</Callout>

**Wedge — pay-by-social. _(Live)_**
Every payment to someone not yet on LitVM pulls a brand-new person onto the network. It's a growth loop that's measurable, not theoretical — adoption built into the core action.

**Moat — the off-ramp. _(Roadmap)_**
Turning USDC into local spending power (mobile money, bank transfer) is the hard, partnership-heavy piece — and it's exactly what makes LitZap an indispensable on/off-ramp for a market rather than one more app. Features get cloned; cash-out rails and partnerships don't. We're honest that this is ahead of us, and it's the make-or-break.

**Magic — the money agent. _(Roadmap)_**
Zapster evolving from a guide into an agent that *moves* money on command — "split this bill," "pay them every Friday," "hold this in escrow until they deliver." We'll claim it only once it executes safely.

## Why Litecoin / LitVM

- Litecoin is a 13-year, globally trusted payments brand — the right rails for small, frequent, cross-border money — and it has had **no modern consumer app**. That seat is open.
- LitVM (LiteForge) gives Litecoin a programmable EVM Layer-2, which is what makes `name.zap` identity, escrow, drops, and future agentic flows possible.

LitZap aims to be the everyday consumer front door of that ecosystem — and a payments + identity layer other LitVM projects can build on.
