# How it works

LitZap turns a few crypto-heavy steps into one smooth flow. Here's what actually happens, end to end.

## Getting started

<Steps>

### Sign in

Choose email, Google, X, or Discord. There's no seed phrase and nothing to install.

### Get your wallet

A secure, self-custodied wallet is created for you automatically the first time you sign in. You control it — LitZap never holds your funds. (See [Architecture](/architecture).)

### Claim your `name.zap`

Pick a handle like `flamingo.zap`. It's registered on-chain and becomes the address people pay you at — and how Zapster knows you.

</Steps>

## Sending money

<Steps>

### Choose how to pay

Pay a `name.zap`, a wallet address, or someone's **X / Discord / email**.

### Enter the amount

Pick **USDC** (the default) or native **zkLTC**, add an optional note.

### Send

- **To a `name.zap` or address** → the payment settles on-chain instantly.
- **To a social handle not yet on LitZap** → the funds are locked in escrow until that person verifies the handle. See [Pay-by-social](/pay-by-social).

</Steps>

## Receiving money

People can pay you several ways, and they all land in the same wallet:

- **Your `name.zap`** — the simplest. "Send it to `flamingo.zap`."
- **Your payment link / QR** — share it anywhere; anyone can pay you, even a first-time user.
- **Your verified X or Discord** — once linked, people can pay your handle directly.

## Claiming a payment sent to your handle

If someone paid your X/Discord/email before you had an account:

<Steps>

### Sign in with that identity

Use the same X / Discord / email the money was sent to (or sign in any way and connect it).

### Verify ownership

LitZap confirms you genuinely own the handle through that account's own login — no passwords are ever shared with us.

### Claim

A **"Money waiting for you"** prompt appears on your home screen. Tap **Claim**, and the funds move into your wallet.

</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  If a social payment is never claimed, it auto-refunds to the sender after the expiry window. No funds get stuck. See [Security &amp; trust](/security).
</Callout>

## Drops

Send money to a whole group at once — a fixed split or a lucky-draw split — and share a single link or code. Great for community rewards, tips, and giveaways.
